The Program

Twelve 50-minute health coaching consultations with personalization, guidance and accountability.

This program redefines nutrition beyond the traditional clinical approach as nourishment from a balance of lifestyle influencers like healthy relationships, a fulfilling career, regular physical activity, and spiritual awareness.

Health Coaching

Health Coaches are knowledgeable advisors who provide ongoing support and guidance as you set goals and make sustainable changes that improve your health and happiness. As your Health Coach, I will listen carefully and help you navigate the world of contradictory nutrition advice to explore what best supports you.

Coaching is a client-based experience, where the coach facilitates the process of the client articulating and moving toward his or her desired goals. A Health Coach simplifies the process for a client to connect the dots between who they are and who they want to be, enabling success in the client’s desired changes.

Your program includes...

  • two 50-minute sessions per month (12 total)
  • email support between sessions
  • recipes that are uncomplicated and healthy
  • coaching to manage the distractions that often prevent the dietary and lifestyle changes you want
  • simple but informative handouts that will increase your wellness knowledge
  • accountability to assist your transformation
  • customized guidance for goals that fit your heartfelt desires
This is a truly customized program that flows with your curiosities, capabilities, and life experiences.

As a client of my health coaching program, you will...

  • strategically set and accomplish empowering goals
  • work to achieve and maintain your ideal weight
  • understand and reduce your cravings
  • increase your energy levels and reduce mental fog
  • feel great in your body
  • explore self-sabotaging habits for long-term success
  • learn about new foods and how you can easily incorporate them
  • improve your personal relationships
  • discover the confidence to create the life you want
  • manage the process of changing habits
  • uncover health food secrets to eat well on a budget
